Number Mine Rules

Two player take turns to pick a number in a given range.

There is a hidden mine inside the range.

The range will shrink according to players' choices.

For example, if the range is (20, 80), one player picks 50, and the mine is 60, the range will shrink to (50, 80).

Whoever steps on the number mine loses.
